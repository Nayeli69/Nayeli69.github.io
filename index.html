Learn to create a Lamp

On this site you can learn how to make a lamp step by step using materials that you can easily acquire. You will learn how to create a lamp in a quick and easy way.

First step

First you must cut the bottle in half with scissors.

Second step

Then you must trace the mold of a butterfly on the pink sheet, but the sheet only in one piece and then you must cut out the butterfly except the center.

Third step

Likewise, in this way it is made with the yellow color, the mold has to be a little smaller and everything must be cut and then joined with the pink color.

Fourth step

Then when we have it ready, it should be glued around the bottle to give it a creative and unique style.

Fifth step

Afterwards, you must open a small hole on both sides of the bottle to tie a piece of stone-colored wool.

Sixth step

After that, the Christmas lights are placed on it, to give it shine.

Seventh step

Lastly, if you want to place drawings inside the butterflies you can do so, but it would already be ready to use.
